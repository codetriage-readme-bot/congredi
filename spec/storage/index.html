"CACHE HAS"

A router (identified by their public key) can declare they have something with packets. We keep track of them in a list of objects totalled like this:

"CACHE PROOF"

A router (identified by their public key) can prove they have something with packets. We keep track of them in a list of objects totalled like this:

(concatenate fields...)

64b key :
    32b lookup hash of bellow :
        32b hash (of content they have)
        32b nonce (as a challenge)
        32b result (hash of adding the nonce to the selected bytes)
        int position (place to grab bytes from)
        datestring (time they proved that nonce)
        32b sig (signature of the above)
